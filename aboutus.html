About us

The company was founded in 1940 by a family that have a big love and passion for coffe. This company have been passed on to generations. It all started in a small farm in Bergen (Norway) The big passion for coffe made the family start their on shop called Conscious Coffee. The goal for Conscious Coffee is to offer a good tasting coffe on the go all around Norway.
